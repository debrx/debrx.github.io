Akoko

A michilen star restaurant!

Akoko is a west african restaurant that blends the freshest seasonal British ingredients with African spices. These West African flavours are inspired by Aji Akokomi's (the founder) heritage

Enjoy exquisite food inside a stylish, minimalist dining room. "The decor takes inspiration from the simplicity of African adobe terracotta clay architecture", says Aji. Designed to evoke a warm and inviting atmosphere

There is an option to book Akoko for a private dining experience. You can enjoy a dedicated team and a menu tailor to your liking.

Menu

Full tasting menus are offered for both lunch and dinner with a shorter menu offered for lunch hours. Drink pairings are offered alongside your meal. Pairing cocktails and wines from across Africa.
