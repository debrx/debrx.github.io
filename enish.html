Enish

A Nigerian Chain!

Enish is a Nigerian that has been serving authentic Nigerian cuisine for over a decade! There are currently 10 striving branches across the UK, two in Dubai, and 1 24-hour location in the UK. The fast pace of these restaurants is something to be in awe of. The first restaurant opened in Lewisham in 2013, 2nd 2016 Finchley Road, 3rd Croyden in 2019, Brixton in 2019 Ilford in 2020 and Camberwell in 2021. After leaving the UK with satisfied appetites in 2019 Enish presence appeared in Houston Texas taking a different approach in Kapricultra Lounge.

February 202 Enish Restaurant took Dubai in style. Located right in the heart of Dubai the restaurant offers a perfect place for both business and social meetings. It serves the true taste of Nigerian cuisine and beverages with a fine dining experience delivering authenticity to the customers' plates.



With the restaurant name coming from the combination of two co-founders it's time we learn more about them.

Eniola and Shola. Enish.

Olushola Medupin is the co-founder and partner of Enish. He started the Enish in 2013 after noticing a gap in the market for high-quality Nigerian food. He grew up in a restaurant run by his mother and grandparents which in turn provided a solid foundation for him to start growing in the food business. One of his beliefs is that food is a powerful path of connecting people and celebrating diversity. He hopes Enish will serve as a way to inspire more people to discover and enjoy Nigerian cuisine.

With the help of his wife, Eniola Medupin, also a co-founder they have combined their expertise to wow the customers with their culinary skills. Eniola oversees operations, finance and business development. As a co-owner, her main responsibilities are overall direction and operational management as well as business development.
