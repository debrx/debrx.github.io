Enish is a Nigerian that has been serving authentic Nigerican cuisne for over a decade! There are currently 10 striving branches across the UK, two in Dubai, and 1 24 hour location in the UK. The fast pace upcoming of these resturants is something to be in awe of. The first resturant opening in Lewisham in 2013, 2nd 2016 Finchley Road, 3rd Croyden 2019, Brixton 2019 Ilford 2020 and Camberwell 2021. After leaving the Uk with satisfied appeties in 2019 enish presence appeared in Houston Texas taking a different approach in Kapricultra Lounge.

Feburuary 202 Enish Resturant took Dubai in style. Located right in the heart of Dubai the resturant offers a perfect place for both buiness and social meetings. It serves the true taste of Nigerian cuisne and beverages with a fine dining experience delivering authenticity to the customers' plates.

With the resturant name coming from the combination of two co-founders it's time we learn more about them.

Eniola and Shola. Enish.

Olushola Medupin is the co founder and partner of Enish. He started the Enish in 2013 after noticing a gap in the market for high quilty Nigerian food. He grew up in a restaurant run by his mother and grandparents which in turn provided a solid foundation for him to start growing in the food buisness. One of his belives is that food is a powerful path of connecting people and celebrating diversity. He hopes Enish will serve as a way to inspire more people to discover and enjoy Nigerian cuisine.

With the help of his wife, Eniola Medupin, also a co-founder they have combined their expertise to wow the customers with their culinary skilss. Eniola oversees operations, finace and business development. As a co-owner her main responsilbilties are overall direction and operational mangement as well as buisness development.
